About

Theme

In celebration of the school's 95th Anniversary, design a future view of the new Block D building that integrates sustainability principles together with the needs in the school and T'oi San community development.

Objectives

  • Understand key Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) relevant to school campus and "T'oi San" community development.
  • Design a new Block D building with sustainability features (e.g., renewable energy, green architecture).
  • Develop data collection, teamwork, and creative problem-solving skills.
  • Present outcomes through poster, video and a web-friendly content package.

Project Proposal Includes

  • Executive Summary of the ProjectA brief overview explaining the goal of the project, the need for a sustainable Block D building, and the main idea behind our design.
  • Design Concept and SDG IntegrationAn introduction to our building design concept and how it supports Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) such as sustainable cities, responsible resource use, and climate action.
  • Floor Plan and Building LayoutDiagrams showing the structure of the building, including the arrangement of classrooms, green spaces, and other functional areas.
  • Sustainability FeaturesKey environmental technologies used in the design, such as solar panels, natural ventilation, green walls, or rainwater collection systems.
  • Data and Survey FindingsCharts and data collected from research or surveys to support our design decisions and demonstrate the potential benefits of the building.
  • Team Roles and ReflectionA summary of each team member's contribution and a reflection on what we learned and the challenges we faced during the project.

Poster Presentation

We created an A2 poster to visually present our building design and sustainability ideas. The poster shows the project title and building name, relevant SDG icons and explanations, architectural design images and floor plans, key sustainability features, and supporting data and charts that explain the benefits of our design.

Short Video Presentation

We produced a 2–3 minute video to introduce our project and design process. The video explains the project goal, the building concept, and the sustainability ideas behind our design. It also includes a showcase of the building model and discusses the expected environmental impact of the proposed Block D building.
